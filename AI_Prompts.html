AI Prompting.

Manual on AI Prompts: Learn how to effectively communicate with AI using clear and precise prompts to get accurate and useful responses.

Researcher: 100621414

What are prompts?

In AI, a prompt is an input, or a set of instructions given to an AI model to get a response. For example, any question you ask to chat is a prompt and any outcome is the response you're looking for. The main friction that comes to prompts is people don’t know how to ask the bot the right questions for the bot to understand or they aren’t clear enough. But as time goes on the language learning models will get better with each iteration. Chat GPT gets better at understanding prompts with the data it's fed and could use this data to predict the next word or what type of response the prompt is looking for.


How AI registers prompts in order

  • The first step is when a user provides a prompt to the AI model. This prompt could be a question, statement, command, or anything that isn’t restricted by the language model. An example a user might use as a prompt is “Tell me a story”.
  • This prompt is received and then processed by the AI model to understand what is being asked and what the desired output is.
  • After the prompt has been contextualized the AI generates a response based on the information the model has learned during its training phase.
  • The last step is the response is delivered to the user this could be in the form of text, image, or a piece of code.

Open-ended prompts

these prompts have a wide range of answers to them and are like open-ended questions that don’t have straightforward answers to them. An example would be “What are your thoughts on climate change?”

Closed-ended prompts

these are prompts that have specific factual answers to them and expect a narrower range of responses for example “What’s the capital of Germany?"

Instructional prompts

these prompts provide instructions or tasks for the AI to perform for example “translate this paragraph to English”.

Contextual prompt

These prompts provide additional context to guide the AI to respond in a contextual way for example you could add brothers and sisters' names for a specific answer regarding the user's needs.

Benefits of prompts in AI

Prompts enable users to interact with AI models more naturally and intuitively making technology more accessible. AI also accepts multiple different prompt types such as code, text, and images which makes AI versatile and gives it many use cases. You could also use Ai for a customized use which could be tailored to the user's needs.


Challenges of prompts in AI

Users could be ambiguous which could lead to unclear or unintended responses. Prompts need to be clear so that the AI can understand what the user needs and wants. Bias Ai could sometimes be trained on biased data which could impact responses to be more biased towards one topic over the other.
