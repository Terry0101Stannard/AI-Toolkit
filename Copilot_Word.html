Microsoft Copilot - Word Integration

Microsoft Copilot is an AI-powered tool designed to enhance productivity across various Microsoft applications such as Word, PowerPoint, Excel, and Teams. As a subscription-based service, Copilot provides access to the most upto date AI technologies, including GPT-4 and GPT-4 Turbo, to streamline your workflow.

Microsoft Word Integration

Upon launching Microsoft Word, you will find two ways to interact with Copilot. You can either click on the Copilot icon located on the left or use the shortcut keys “Alt + I” to start drafting with the AI. This small icon will persist on every new line throughout the document for quick access.

Content Formatting

Copilot has the capability to transform an informal sentence or paragraph into a formal one. To use this feature, highlight the text you want to rewrite, then click on the Copilot icon on the left of the page next to the text. Select “rewrite with Copilot” from the options.

You can fine-tune the formality level of the rewritten text. For this example, we’ll choose “Professional”. Click on “Regenerate” to rewrite the sentence in this style.

The output from this is: “Hello everyone, I kindly ask that we all show more care for each other and tidy up the break room after using it. It’s not reasonable to expect someone else to handle your dirty dishes, wrappers, and crumbs. The break room is a common area, so let’s maintain it clean and courteous. Thank you!”

One of the most powerful features that Copilot can offer is the ability to summarize your document and provide an outline of the main points. To use this feature, click on Copilot on the ribbon and select "summarize document". You can choose how long you want the summary to be and whether you want it to include keywords or not. Copilot will analyze your document and generate a concise summary that highlights the key information and arguments. You can use this summary to review your document, share it with others, or create an abstract or executive summary. Copilot can help you save time and effort while working with long and complex documents.
